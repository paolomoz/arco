Is the Arco Doppio right for you?

The Doppio is where most people land when they outgrow their first machine. It solves the two biggest complaints about entry-level espresso — temperature inconsistency and the wait between brewing and steaming. But it also asks more of you than a simpler machine does.

Perfect For

  • You have been using a single-boiler machine for six months or more and are tired of temperature surfing and waiting between shots and steaming.
  • You make milk drinks daily and want to brew and steam at the same time without compromise.
  • You have developed enough technique to appreciate PID temperature control but don't need pressure profiling yet.
  • You want a machine that pairs well with a stepless grinder and gives you room to grow for several years.

Not Ideal For

  • You are brand new to espresso and haven't yet learned basic workflow — the Doppio's adjustable OPV and dual PID are wasted if you are still learning to tamp consistently.
  • You are already experimenting with pressure profiling or flow control — the Doppio doesn't offer pre-infusion or profiling, and you will feel limited quickly.
  • You want a machine that heats up in under a minute — the dual boilers need 3 minutes, which is fast for this class but slow compared to a thermoblock.

Common Questions

Is the jump from €299 to €549 worth it?
If you drink straight espresso only and never make milk drinks, the Primo might still be enough for you. The Doppio's biggest advantages are simultaneous brew/steam, PID stability, and the adjustable OPV. If any of those matter to your daily routine, the €250 gap pays for itself in the first month of better mornings.
Should I skip the Doppio and go straight to the Studio?
Only if you are confident you will use pre-infusion control and can commit to an 8-minute warm-up. The Doppio is not a compromise — it is a genuinely excellent machine with a faster warm-up and less operational complexity. Many experienced baristas use dual-boiler machines like the Doppio as their daily driver.
Can it handle hosting — making coffee for four or five guests?
Yes, comfortably. The dual boiler system means you don't have to wait between shots and steaming, and the 2-litre water tank handles multiple drinks without refilling. It is not an office machine, but it handles a dinner party with ease.
