Description
The Arco Zero is a single-dose grinder built on a simple principle: what goes in must come out. Its 64mm titanium-coated flat burrs produce an exceptionally uniform particle distribution with virtually zero retention — weigh your beans in, and the same weight comes out as ground coffee. The bellows purge clears the last fraction of a gram from the grinding chamber, ensuring that every shot represents only the beans you chose for it. Stepless micrometric adjustment, a low-RPM motor that minimises heat transfer, and an anti-static outlet complete a grinder that leaves nothing to chance. The Zero is not the grinder for everyone. It is the grinder for people who measure everything.

Perfect for single-dosing purists who rotate beans frequently.

The Zero was designed for baristas who buy multiple bags at once and want to switch between them without cross-contamination. Weigh a dose, grind it, purge with the bellows, and load a completely different origin — with no flavour carryover from the previous shot.
