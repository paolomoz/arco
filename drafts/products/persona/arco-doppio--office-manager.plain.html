Premium espresso for small teams, without the commercial price tag.

The Doppio's dual-boiler system handles back-to-back drinks for a team of 5 to 10 -- real espresso and milk drinks, ready fast, built to last.

For a small office or studio, the Doppio hits a sweet spot that commercial machines and pod systems both miss. Its dual-boiler architecture means someone can pull a shot while another steams milk -- no waiting, no queuing. The rotary pump handles back-to-back extractions without pressure drops, and the 2.5-litre tank gets a small team through the morning without constant refilling. Unlike pod machines, the Doppio uses standard ground coffee or fresh beans, which means dramatically lower per-cup costs and dramatically better coffee. Unlike full commercial equipment, it fits on a standard countertop, runs on a standard outlet, and costs a fraction to maintain. For teams of 5 to 10 who care about quality, this is the most cost-effective way to keep everyone caffeinated and happy.
