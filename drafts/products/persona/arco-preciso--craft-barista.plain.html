48mm conicals tuned for flavour clarity on medium-dark roasts.

The Preciso isn't trying to be a flat-burr competitor. It's a purpose-built conical grinder that excels where conicals should: sweetness, body, and a forgiving extraction window on traditional and medium-dark profiles.

You already know the flat-vs-conical debate, so here's where the Preciso lands: 48mm steel conical burrs running at 600 RPM, producing a bimodal distribution that naturally emphasises sweetness and body. This makes it exceptional for medium to dark roasts where you want round, chocolatey flavours without the aggressive clarity of a unimodal flat profile. The 40-step macro adjustment with continuous micro dial gives you enough precision to dial in origin-specific profiles without the finicky overshoot of a fully stepless mechanism. Retention at under 0.8 grams is respectable -- not single-dose territory, but with a quick purge you're within half a gram. The 250g hopper supports a hopper-flow workflow for daily use. If your rotation includes traditional Italian blends, natural-process Brazils, or anything in the medium-dark spectrum, the Preciso is a dedicated tool that does exactly what conicals do best.
