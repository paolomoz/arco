Description
The Arco Primo is designed for those who want genuine espresso without a steep learning curve. Its thermoblock heating system reaches brewing temperature in just 90 seconds, so your morning never has to wait. The standard 58mm portafilter means you can use commercial-grade baskets and accessories as your skills grow. Clean lines, a small footprint, and an intuitive interface make the Primo the quiet centrepiece of any kitchen counter.
