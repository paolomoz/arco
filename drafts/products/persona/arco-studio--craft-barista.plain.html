Flow profiling. Saturated group. Full control.

Dual stainless boilers, a rotary pump with integrated flow control, shot timer, and pressure gauge -- the Studio is a prosumer machine built for baristas who want to manipulate every variable.

The Studio is where Arco stops holding your hand. The saturated 58mm group head delivers thermal stability measured in tenths of a degree. The rotary pump feeds through an integrated flow control valve that lets you manipulate flow rate in real time -- ramp pre-infusion, hold pressure, decline through the tail of the shot. Dual stainless steel boilers (300ml brew, 500ml steam) are independently PID-controlled, and the built-in shot timer syncs with the pump so you're never guessing. The 3-litre tank supports reservoir operation or direct plumbing for zero-interruption workflows. At 18 kg, it's substantial enough for thermal mass, compact enough for a home counter. This is the machine you buy when you've stopped asking how to make espresso and started asking what espresso can become.
