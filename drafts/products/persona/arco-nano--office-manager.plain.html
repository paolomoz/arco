Personal desk espresso. No shared kitchen required.

The Arco Nano is small enough to sit on a desk or credenza, quiet enough for an open office, and makes real espresso that puts the communal pod machine to shame.

Not every office has a kitchen. Not every kitchen has a machine worth using. The Nano solves both problems by going directly to your desk. At 4.2 kg and 18 x 28 x 25 cm, it takes up less space than a monitor stand. The thermoblock is near-silent during operation -- no one in the open plan will notice. The 1-litre tank gets you through 8 to 10 shots before needing a refill, and the drip tray is easy to empty at any sink. Per-cup cost with quality beans and a hand grinder runs about 10 to 15 cents -- a fraction of the pod machine in the break room, and incomparably better. If you're the person in the office who actually cares about coffee, the Nano is your secret weapon.
