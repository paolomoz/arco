Espresso Anywhere

You refuse to let geography dictate the quality of your coffee. Whether you are in a hotel room in Tokyo, a campsite in Patagonia, or a co-working space in Lisbon, you believe great espresso should travel with you. Hotel pod machines and airport lattes are not an option. You carry your standards in your bag, right alongside your passport.

The Espresso Anywhere experience is designed for travelers, remote workers, and anyone whose life does not stay in one place for long. Your gear needs to be light, durable, and capable of producing real espresso without relying on electricity or plumbed water. It needs to fit in a carry-on and survive the bumps of transit. And it needs to be simple enough to use when you are jet-lagged and operating on autopilot.

The Arco Viaggio is the heart of this setup. A manual lever portable that generates full espresso pressure through a hand-operated piston, it weighs under 400 grams and fits in the palm of your hand. Pair it with a quality hand grinder and a compact travel scale, and you have a self-contained espresso station that goes wherever you go. Add a few pre-dosed containers of your favorite beans, and you are set for a week on the road without compromise.
