The Non-Barista

You love great coffee. You just do not love the process of making it. The weighing, the tamping, the timing, the cleaning, the dialing in every time you open a new bag. You have friends who geek out over extraction percentages and grind microns, and you are happy for them. But for you, the best espresso machine is the one that delivers a perfect cup when you press a single button.

The Non-Barista experience is for people who refuse to compromise on quality but have zero interest in becoming a home barista. You want the taste of specialty coffee without the learning curve, the ritual, or the counter full of accessories. You want to pour beans into a hopper, press start, and walk away knowing that what lands in your cup is genuinely good, not just convenient.

The Arco Automatico was designed with you in mind. It combines a built-in conical burr grinder, automated dosing, tamping, and extraction, and a self-cleaning cycle that runs at the touch of a button. The machine's software adjusts grind and extraction parameters based on the beans you load, using pre-programmed profiles for light, medium, and dark roasts. The result is consistent, high-quality espresso that requires nothing from you except choosing your beans and rinsing the drip tray. Pair it with the Arco Cleaning Kit for quarterly deep cleaning, and your Automatico will deliver outstanding coffee for years with minimal effort.
